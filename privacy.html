Last updated: June 2026

At Simple Gamez, accessible from simplegamez.com, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Simple Gamez and how we use it.

Consent

By using our website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.

Information We Collect

Simple Gamez is a static HTML5 games website. We do not require you to create an account, register, or provide personal information (such as your name or email address) to play our games.

However, like most websites, we collect certain non-personally identifiable data automatically:

  • High Scores & Game Progress: Your high scores, stardust balance, unlocked skins, and gameplay statistics are stored locally on your device using your browser's local storage (`localStorage`) or IndexedDB. We do not transmit, share, or store this data on our servers.
  • Log Files: Simple Gamez follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

Google AdSense & DoubleClick Cookie

Google is one of the third-party vendors on our site. It also uses cookies, known as DART cookies, to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to simplegamez.com and other sites on the internet.

Visitors may choose to decline the use of DART cookies by visiting the Google ad and content network Privacy Policy at the following URL: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads
